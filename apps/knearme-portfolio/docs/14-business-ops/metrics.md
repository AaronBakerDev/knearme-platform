# Business Metrics

This document defines the business metrics that drive priorities across lanes.

## North Star

Pick one core metric and align all lanes to it.

Suggested options (choose one):
- Published projects per contractor per month
- Contractors who publish within 7 days of signup
- Jobs won attributable to KnearMe portfolios

## Company Health Metrics

- Active contractors (weekly/monthly)
- Retention (cohort-based)
- Gross margin
- Support volume and resolution time
- Product quality (defect rate, incident count)

## Lane Metrics (Leading Indicators)

### Product & Engineering
- Time to first published project
- Feature adoption per release
- Defect rate per release

### Growth & Marketing
- Qualified leads per week
- Cost per qualified lead
- Content conversion rate

### Sales
- Win rate
- Time-to-close
- Pipeline coverage

### Customer Success
- 7-day activation rate
- Churn rate
- NPS or CSAT

### Operations
- Process completion rate
- Incidents per month
- Manual hours reduced

### Finance & Legal
- Gross margin
- Billing accuracy
- Cash runway

### People
- Time-to-hire
- Onboarding completion
- Retention
