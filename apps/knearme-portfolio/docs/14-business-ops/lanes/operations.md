# Lane: Operations

## Purpose

Run the business efficiently, reliably, and with clear processes.

## Owner

- Primary: Ops lead
- Backup: Engineering lead

## Inputs

- Workflow needs from all lanes
- System reliability data
- Tooling gaps

## Outputs

- SOPs and runbooks
- Automation and tooling
- Risk mitigation and compliance routines

## KPIs

- Process completion rate
- Incident response time
- Tooling uptime
- Manual hours reduced

## Cadence

- Weekly ops review
- Monthly process audit
- Quarterly risk assessment

## Artifacts

- SOP library
- Runbooks
- Tooling inventory

## Escalations

- Critical process failures
- Repeated manual errors
- Compliance risk

## Dependencies

- Product for system changes
- Finance for budget
- People for onboarding and training
