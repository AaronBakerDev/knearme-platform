# Lane: People & Recruiting

## Purpose

Build the right team and keep people aligned and effective.

## Owner

- Primary: People lead
- Backup: Ops lead

## Inputs

- Hiring needs from lanes
- Budget constraints
- Performance feedback

## Outputs

- Hiring plan
- Onboarding and training
- Role clarity and accountability

## KPIs

- Time-to-hire
- Onboarding completion
- Team retention
- Role clarity score (internal survey)

## Cadence

- Monthly hiring review
- Quarterly org design review
- Annual compensation review

## Artifacts

- Role definitions
- Onboarding checklists
- Performance review templates

## Escalations

- Key role gaps
- Performance issues with business impact
- Capacity bottlenecks

## Dependencies

- Finance for budgets
- Product for technical roles
- Ops for onboarding systems
