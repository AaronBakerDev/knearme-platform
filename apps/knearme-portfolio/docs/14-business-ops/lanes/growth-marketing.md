# Lane: Growth & Marketing

## Purpose

Drive qualified contractor demand and make KnearMe visible where contractors look.

## Owner

- Primary: Growth lead
- Backup: Product lead

## Inputs

- Product positioning and differentiation
- Performance data from acquisition channels
- Customer stories and proof

## Outputs

- Acquisition strategy and campaigns
- Content and distribution plans
- Channel performance reporting

## KPIs

- Qualified leads per week
- Cost per qualified lead
- Conversion rate to activation
- Content performance (CTR, engagement)

## Cadence

- Weekly channel review
- Monthly campaign planning
- Quarterly brand/positioning review

## Artifacts

- Messaging guide
- Content calendar
- Channel dashboards

## Escalations

- Sudden drop in qualified leads
- Channel compliance or account risk
- Messaging misalignment with product

## Dependencies

- Product for proof and capabilities
- Sales for lead quality feedback
- Support for customer insights
