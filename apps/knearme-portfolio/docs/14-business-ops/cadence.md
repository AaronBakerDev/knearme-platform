# Operating Cadence

## Weekly

- Metrics review (North Star + lane KPIs)
- Product shipping check
- Support trend review
- Sales pipeline check
- Growth channel health

## Monthly

- Roadmap and priorities review
- Pricing and packaging review
- Hiring needs check
- Ops process audit

## Quarterly

- Strategy review
- Architecture review
- Budget and runway review
- Lane goals reset

## Decision Rhythm

- Fast decisions: daily, owner-only
- Medium decisions: weekly, cross-lane
- High-impact decisions: quarterly, leadership sign-off
