# kNearme Content Planning Guide

## Purpose
This repository serves as a central place for planning and organizing content for kNearme - a platform that helps contractors showcase their completed projects while also providing valuable evergreen content for homeowners and property managers.

## Directory Structure
- `/chimney` - Content planning for chimney services
- `/masonry` - Content planning for masonry services
- `/concrete` - Content planning for concrete services
- `/evergreen` - General informational content not specific to a service category

## Content Planning Process
1. **Research**: Begin with keyword research and competitor analysis for each service category
2. **Topic Selection**: Identify high-value topics based on search volume and relevance
3. **Content Briefs**: Create detailed briefs for each piece of content
4. **Content Calendar**: Schedule publication dates for content
5. **Portfolio Examples**: Compile examples of quality project showcases for contractors

## Content Types
1. **Service Pages**: Detailed descriptions of specific services
2. **How-to Guides**: Step-by-step instructions for DIY projects
3. **Maintenance Tips**: Regular maintenance advice for homeowners
4. **Hiring Guides**: How to find and evaluate contractors
5. **Project Showcases**: Examples of completed projects with details
6. **Problem-Solution Content**: Common issues and how contractors solve them

## Best Practices
- Focus on local SEO terms when applicable
- Include contractor benefits for each content type
- Ensure content includes clear CTAs for both homeowners and contractors
- Use high-quality images with proper alt text
- Structure content with proper H1, H2, H3 tags for readability and SEO

## Target Audience
1. **Contractors**: Looking to showcase their work and find new clients
2. **Homeowners**: Seeking reliable contractors or DIY information
3. **Property Managers**: Managing maintenance for multiple properties

## Success Metrics
- Organic traffic growth
- Contractor signups
- Project showcase submissions
- Engagement with content (time on page, comments)
- Conversion rates (contact form submissions, contractor inquiries)
