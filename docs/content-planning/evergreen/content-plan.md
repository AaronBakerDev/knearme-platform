# Evergreen Content Plan

## Purpose
This plan outlines general home improvement and contractor-selection content that remains valuable year-round and is not specific to any single service category. This content helps establish kNearme as an authoritative resource for homeowners while driving organic traffic and supporting our contractor partners.

- Finding local contractors
- Home renovation guide
- Contractor reviews
