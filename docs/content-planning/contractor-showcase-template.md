# Contractor Showcase Template

## Overview
This template provides a standardized format for creating high-quality contractor showcase articles that highlight kNearme professionals, their expertise, and completed projects. These showcases serve dual purposes: promoting our contractor partners while providing valuable content that demonstrates quality workmanship for potential clients.

## Basic Information
- **Title Format**: [Contractor Name]: [Specialty] Excellence in [Location]
- **Type**: Contractor Showcase / Project Spotlight
- **Word Count**: 1,000-1,500 words
- **Categories**: Service-specific (Chimney, Masonry, Concrete) + Contractor Stories
- **Keywords**: Contractor name, service keywords, location keywords, project type keywords

## Content Structure

### Contractor Introduction (~150 words)
- Company name and location
- Years of experience and specialization
- Any notable certifications, accreditations, or awards
- Brief company history or founding story
- Service area coverage

### Meet the Team (~150 words)
- Brief introduction to owner/key team members
- Team expertise and specializations
- Combined years of experience
- Training and certification information
- Company values and workmanship philosophy

### Services Spotlight (~150 words)
- Overview of main services offered
- Specialization areas
- Unique or signature services
- Quality standards and guarantees
- Service process overview

### Project Showcase #1: [Project Type] (~250 words)
- Project overview and client needs
- Challenges faced and creative solutions
- Materials and techniques used
- Timeline and process highlights
- Before and after description
- Client testimonial (if available)

### Project Showcase #2: [Project Type] (~250 words)
- Different project type from first showcase
- Unique challenges or requirements
- Special techniques or materials
- Results and benefits to the client
- Before and after description
- Client testimonial (if available)

### Industry Expertise (~200 words)
- Expert tips related to their specialty
- Common issues they encounter and how they solve them
- Seasonal advice for homeowners
- Maintenance recommendations
- Signs homeowners should watch for

### Client Success Stories (~150 words)
- Brief stories of successful projects
- Client feedback highlights
- Recurring themes in positive reviews
- Examples of going above and beyond
- Long-term client relationships

### Conclusion & Contact Information (~100 words)
- Summary of contractor's unique value
- Service area reminder
- Contact information
- Link to their kNearme profile
- Call to action for project inquiries

## Visual Elements
- Company logo and team photo
- Before/after photos of showcased projects (minimum 4 pairs)
- Action shots of team working on projects
- Close-up details of quality workmanship
- Video testimonial or project walkthrough (if available)

## Contractor Information Requirements
Request the following from each featured contractor:
- Company logo (high resolution)
- Team/owner photos
- 5-10 high-quality before/after project photos
- Complete service list
- Service area (cities/counties covered)
- Certifications and licensing information
- 2-3 client testimonials with permission to publish
- Years in business and company history
- Specializations and unique services

## Showcase Selection Criteria
Contractors featured in showcases should meet these minimum requirements:
- Complete kNearme profile with all services listed
- Minimum of 5 high-quality project photos
- Proper licensing and insurance verified
- At least 3 verified customer reviews
- In business for minimum of 3 years
- Demonstrated expertise in specialty area
- Responsive to communication requests
- Willingness to provide additional information for feature

## Publication Guidelines
- Schedule contractor showcases to rotate between service categories
- Aim for 2-4 contractor showcases per month
- Feature contractors in different geographic regions
- Coordinate publication with seasonal service relevance
- Promote showcases via contractor's own channels
- Include in relevant service category newsletters

## SEO Considerations
- Use contractor name in title, URL, and H1
- Include location-specific keywords
- Incorporate relevant service category keywords
- Add schema markup for local business
- Include alt text on all images
- Create custom meta description highlighting expertise

## Promotion Plan
- Share on kNearme social media channels
- Create social graphics with key project photos
- Email showcase to contractor for their own promotion
- Include in monthly homeowner newsletter
- Add to relevant service category pages
- Feature in "Top Contractors" roundups by category
