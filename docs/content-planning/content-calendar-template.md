## Content Performance Metrics

### Success Metrics by Content Type
- **Service Pages**: Conversion rate, time on page, bounce rate
- **Educational Content**: Organic traffic, page views, social shares
- **Problem-Solution Content**: Organic traffic, engagement metrics
- **Project Showcases**: Engagement metrics, contractor inquiries
- **Contractor Features**: Referral traffic, contractor inquiries
- **Evergreen Guides**: Organic traffic, time on page, backlinks

### Monthly Reporting
Track the following metrics for all published content:
- Organic traffic
- Conversion rate
- Social shares
- Backlinks
- Average time on page
- Bounce rate
- Engagement (comments, interactions)
- Contractor inquiries
- Search ranking for target keywords

## Content Planning Checklist

### Monthly Planning (15th of Previous Month)
- [ ] Review upcoming seasonal relevance
- [ ] Check keyword performance data
- [ ] Gather contractor input and materials
- [ ] Finalize content topics
- [ ] Assign content to team members
- [ ] Update content calendar
- [ ] Distribute to all stakeholders

### Weekly Review
- [ ] Confirm content is on schedule
- [ ] Review performance of recently published content
- [ ] Make adjustments to upcoming content as needed
- [ ] Update status in content calendar
- [ ] Communicate any changes to team

## Notes on Content Balance
- Maintain equal focus across chimney, masonry, and concrete service categories
- Include at least one evergreen guide per month
- Feature 2-4 contractor showcases monthly
- Balance educational, service, problem-solution, and showcase content
- Align content with seasonal relevance and search trends
