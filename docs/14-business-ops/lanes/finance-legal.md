# Lane: Finance & Legal

## Purpose

Ensure sustainable economics, clean books, and legal safety.

## Owner

- Primary: Finance lead
- Backup: Operations lead

## Inputs

- Revenue and cost data
- Pricing and packaging updates
- Contract and compliance needs

## Outputs

- Pricing strategy and unit economics
- Billing processes and policies
- Contracts and compliance guidance

## KPIs

- Gross margin
- Cash runway
- Billing accuracy
- Risk/compliance incidents

## Cadence

- Monthly financial review
- Quarterly pricing review
- Annual compliance audit

## Artifacts

- Pricing model
- Billing policy
- Contract templates

## Escalations

- Large variance in revenue or costs
- Contract exceptions
- Compliance or regulatory risk

## Dependencies

- Sales for pricing feedback
- Ops for billing processes
- Product for feature-driven pricing
