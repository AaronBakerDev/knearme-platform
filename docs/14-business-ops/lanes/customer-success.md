# Lane: Customer Success & Support

## Purpose

Ensure contractors succeed, stay active, and advocate for KnearMe.

## Owner

- Primary: Success lead
- Backup: Product lead

## Inputs

- Support tickets and feedback
- Usage data and churn signals
- Onboarding progress

## Outputs

- Resolved tickets and knowledge base
- Onboarding improvements
- Churn risk flags

## KPIs

- First project published within 7 days
- Ticket resolution time
- Retention / churn rate
- NPS or customer satisfaction

## Cadence

- Weekly support trend review
- Monthly onboarding review
- Quarterly success playbook update

## Artifacts

- Onboarding checklist
- Support macros and playbooks
- Feedback summaries

## Escalations

- Security/privacy concerns
- Billing disputes
- High-value churn risk

## Dependencies

- Product for feature gaps
- Sales for handoffs
- Ops for tooling and workflows
