# Lane: Product & Engineering

## Purpose

Deliver a reliable product that proves contractor work and helps them win more jobs.

## Owner

- Primary: Product/Engineering lead
- Backup: Operations lead

## Inputs

- Customer feedback and support trends
- Growth and sales insights
- Strategic goals from leadership

## Outputs

- Shipped features and fixes
- Clear roadmap and release notes
- Stable, scalable architecture

## KPIs

- Activation: time to first published project
- Retention: weekly active contractors
- Quality: defect rate, incident rate
- Delivery: cycle time, release frequency

## Cadence

- Weekly planning + release check
- Monthly roadmap review
- Quarterly architecture review

## Artifacts

- Product roadmap (docs/10-launch or internal backlog)
- Architecture docs (docs/03-architecture)
- Decision records (docs/05-decisions)

## Escalations

- Security vulnerabilities
- Repeated customer-impacting bugs
- Architecture changes with long-term risk

## Dependencies

- Growth for positioning and use cases
- Support for pain points and bug triage
- Ops for reliability and tooling
