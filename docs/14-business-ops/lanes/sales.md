# Lane: Sales

## Purpose

Convert qualified contractors into paying customers and expand usage.

## Owner

- Primary: Sales lead
- Backup: Growth lead

## Inputs

- Qualified leads
- Pricing and packaging
- Product proof points

## Outputs

- Closed-won customers
- Objection insights
- Forecast and pipeline health

## KPIs

- Win rate
- Time-to-close
- ARPU / ACV
- Pipeline coverage

## Cadence

- Weekly pipeline review
- Monthly pricing feedback loop
- Quarterly segment focus

## Artifacts

- Sales scripts and discovery guides
- Objection handling library
- Pipeline dashboard

## Escalations

- Enterprise legal/procurement
- Pricing exceptions
- High-risk churn signals

## Dependencies

- Growth for lead flow
- Product for capabilities + roadmap
- Finance for pricing and contracts
